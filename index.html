Frequently Asked Questions

Far far away, behind the word mountains, far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts.

What is a hackathon?

Hackathon is an event, typically lasting several days, in which a large number of programmers meet to engage in collaborative computer programming .

Who can come?

Zero to One Hackathon applications are open to any enrolled college student, from all over India.

What does it cost?

Zero to One Hackathon costs fees of 150/participant . We provide meals, caffeine, swag, workspace, and WiFi for the event.

What should i bring?

All you need is a valid student ID and a laptop. We provide the rest! (A change of clothes and a pillow and/or sleeping bag is recommended, though.)

About CESS

The engine which drives the CET department towards excellence under the guidance of Head of Department Dr. Sandeep Sharma. The aim is to modify the students to extraordinary individuals, increasing their technical skills, experience along with imbibing in them the practical skills and aspects such as integrity, team spirit, cooperation which will help to merge and contribute much easily in the corporate world, above all broadening their perspective. CESS has different teams focusing primarily on the skills such as coding, public speaking, drawing and design, marketing and promotions, thus developing in students the skills imperative in the real world. Several events are organised by CESS throughout the year such as workshops, seminars, techfest enhance technical knowledge and skills, giving the students necessary knowledge and guidance for future endeavours.
